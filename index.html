How to Take It

  1. Open the wax shell: press until light crack lines appear, then split along the crack.
  2. Take out the inner pill: open the inner plastic cap and remove any wrapping film.
  3. Take the pill: chew first, then swallow with warm water. Split into smaller pieces if needed.

Looped visual: press and crack -> open and remove -> chew and drink warm water.

Note: Do not swallow a whole large honey pill directly.
